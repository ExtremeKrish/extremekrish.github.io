How To Upload Images on Internet and get its URL from your phone

By: Aakanksha ğŸ’•
Go To Play Store Search For Dropbox
Install Dropbox & Open it
click on + button to upload a photo/video or any other files you wish.
Select Upload Photos & Videos
choose your photos & videos & upload it.
After upload select menu
Select Copy Link
We have copied the link
Now we've this in our clipboard
https://www.dropbox.com/s/i15e3biaa94la3f/teddy.jpg?dl=0
Now we need to modify link like this:
https://dl.dropbox.com/s/i15e3biaa94la3f/teddy.jpg
Replace: www with dl & Remove: ?dl=0
Now we can use this link in src
<img src = "https://dl.dropbox.com/s/i15e3biaa94la3f/teddy.jpg" />
